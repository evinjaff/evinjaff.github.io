Laser Cutting

Using a big scary laser to cut wood

Why do I think Laser Cutting is Cool?

Well first off, laser cutting looks cool, as demonstrated by the GIF below, but I think that the best part about laser cutting is the aesthetic that materials cut by lasers give off. Laser cut wood looks fantastic: you get sharp edges, black sides, and a distinct smell that lets you know that a big scary laser cut that wood! And what's even cooler is that laser cutters can also engrave designs on top of the material, which allows you to engrave your images, text, and other printable content onto wood, metal, or other materials!
