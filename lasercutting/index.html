Laser Cutting

Using a big scary laser to cut wood

Why do I think Laser Cutting is Cool?

Well first off, laser cutting looks really cool, as demonstrated by the GIF bellow, but it is also really cool because of the aesthetic that wood and materials cut by lasers gives off. You get sharp edges, burnt sides, and I believe that laser cutters hold the record for the smallest kerf (amount of material removed by cutting) of a wood-cutting tool. Also, there is a large following of people who use laser cutting for creating art.
