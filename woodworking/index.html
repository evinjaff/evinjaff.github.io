Why do I like woodworking?

No reason really, one day I wanted to make something and it needed to be in wood, so I bought some wood and a basic saw to cut the wood and from there, I wanted to make more stuff. I feel like I've always used woodworking as a way to make something, as in I get an idea and I then think 'Oh, I should make that in wood'.
