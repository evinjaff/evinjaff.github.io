The Evolution of the Logo

Winter 010: Fun with MS Paint

The first stab at making a logo was what the 1960's cyberman in Doctor Who look like, really horrible but at the time I thought I was fine. Of course, you have to give an 11 yeer old some credit for trying!

Fall 2015: Mandatory Logo!

The original reason why I revived the Starkomon name was because I needed a fake company name when I was signing up for an AWS account. And since "Company McCompanyFace" was taken, I went with Starkomon Innovations with a quickly doctored up logo.

Summer 2016: School Reunion!

That coming Summer, with the Starkomon name fresh in the head, I met up with my Elementary School Starkomon Executives for a school reunion and we got together and made an updated version of the logo for fun. Bonus Fact: I also used this for a full sized logo for my company on my AWS account.

Fall 2018: Breath of the Wild Inspiration!

As a big Zelda fan, I loved the Sheikah aesthetic of the font and decided to vector design some of the typography in the same style to use for an updated logo if and when I need it.

Some things we made

Even though all my friends and I originally created Starkomon to signify we would stay friends after we went to different schools, we still ended up making a lot of things that were impressive for our young ages.

Winter 2010: Starko Notes by Evin and Charlie

Charlie and I came up with the idea of Starko Notes, printed documents chock-full of video game tips and trivia that we then printed on a school copier to sell to fellow students in exchange for their desserts and snacks in their school lunch; we were quite the young entrepreneurs (or conpeople), and generated a fortune of chocolate milk and brownies until our catholic school teachers overlords found out about our plan.

Spring 2010: Starko Comics by Jason and Evin

Intent on continuing to sell our publications for school snacks, we waited for a few months and then went on to draw and make comics of our favorite video game and movie characters to secretly distribute and sell for more snacks.
