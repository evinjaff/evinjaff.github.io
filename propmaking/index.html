What do I love propmaking?

I love the props and weapons in movies and TV. It's awesome to see the Obi Wan-Kenobi wield a killer laser sword, or see Thor summon a thunderstorm using his hammer. After watching the shows, however, I want those props even more, becuase of how cool they are! Even more, I love bringing some of their functionality into the world. Sure, I can't summon thunderstorms with my Thor's hammer, but I can put a souped up bug zapper inside the hammer to explode soda cans!
